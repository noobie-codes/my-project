Realizing the need of an institution that can provide young students with quality and value oriented education, The Chandbagh School was established in 1996 by Chaudhary Education Foundatioin, a sister concern of the Chaudhary Group (CG). At present, the school is managed by CG|Education.

The Chandbagh School is a co-educational school, located in Bansbari, Kathmandu, adjacent to Shahid Gangalal National Heart Centre . It is located in a peaceful area of nearly 50,000 sq. ft.

The School was initially a Primary Day Boarding School, but it is a full-fledged Secondary and Higher Secondary School now , having its affiliation with SEE/NEB Board of Nepal and CBSE. India. The Chandbagh School is a much soughtafter premier school in Kathmandu, Nepal.
